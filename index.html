About RGIPT Sports Council

The RGIPT Sports Council is the backbone of all sporting activities at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology. It promotes a culture of fitness, sportsmanship, and teamwork by organizing events, tournaments, and training sessions. The council manages both indoor and outdoor sports, encouraging students to excel in football, basketball, cricket, badminton, volleyball, lawn tennis, athletics, as well as indoor games like carrom, chess, and table tennis. With a vision to build leadership and sports spirit, the council is dedicated to fostering talent and unity among students.
